Travelers, however, should be aware of its specific baggage allowance rules. According to the Frontier Airlines Baggage Allowance​ 2025 terms, carry-on as well as checked bags are charged separately. 1 complimentary personal item like a tiny backpack is included in every ticket. Nevertheless, there are certain restrictions on both checked and carry-on luggage. If these limits are surpassed, then there can be further costs. To avoid these expenses, it is suggested to abide by all the rules of baggage policy of this airline in advance. Accordingly, when necessary, purchase extra baggage allowance online to receive some discounts. Frontier Airline Carry on Baggage Allowance​Travelers can bring 1 personal item on board. This consists of a briefcase, diaper bag, computer bag, handbag, totes, or children’s backpack. The maximum dimensions of a personal item, including handles, straps, and wheels, is 14” H* 18” W* 8” D. In addition to this, a traveler can bring 1 carry-on luggage on board. This includes a compact suitcase, wheeled luggage, a big backpack, or a tiny duffel bag. Frontier Airlines allows a maximum of 24″ H* 16″ W* 10″ D in carry-on luggage. Additionally, the combined weight of the handles, wheels, and straps is less than 35 lbs. What is Frontier Airlines Checked Baggage Allowance?The maximum Frontier Airlines international baggage allowance​ is 62 linear inches. Furthermore, the maximum amount of checked baggage should be forty pounds. Additional fees will apply for bags weighing 41 to 100 pounds. Overweight bags will incur an extra fee for bags that are beyond these restrictions. Further, each oversized luggage will incur a separate cost. Furthermore, Frontier Airlines does not permit luggage that weighs more than 110 pounds.Note: Bags longer than 110 linear inches will not be accepted as per the rules of Frontier Airlines baggage allowance​ 2025. Is Smart Luggage Allowed on Frontier Airlines Flights?Smart bags are usually hard-shell cases with integrated electronics for 1 or more uses. The batteries that power these bags are lithium-ion (Li-ion). Considering these, for the sake of everyone’s safety, including the workers, Frontier Airlines does not permit smart bags on its airplanes in the circumstances listed below. When it is impossible to take the battery out of the bag. In case the battery has more than 100 watt-hours. When there are indications of any damage or flaw in the battery. To guarantee the battery is handled and transported safely, it is important to specify the battery’s type and size when checking in. The following guidelines are applicable for taking the battery on board: Before boarding the flight, the battery needs to be taken out. After being taken out, the battery needs to be put safely inside a carry-on luggage. According to the rules of Frontier Airlines international baggage allowance​, for infants, travelers can bring a second complimentary personal item on flights. This is inclusive of a diaper bag. It should be under 8*8*14 inches. In addition, infants traveling in strollers are permitted to enter the Frontier boarding gate. Here, this air carrier will provide a complimentary check-in. Ramp agents will return the stroller to fliers so that they can use it at the airport when they get there. The following items are free to bring on board as well: CRS or Child Restraint System: Infant and child restraint systems that have been authorized for use on airplanes are accepted by Frontier Airlines. With some special guidelines of Frontier Airlines baggage allowance 2025​, it must fit into the appropriate airplane seat with the armrest down. Child Harness: Frontier Airlines allows the FAA-approved AMSafe Aviation C.A.R.E.S child harness equipment on board. It is intended for kids who weigh between 22 and 44 pounds (10 to 20 kg). In addition, it needs to be marked “FAA Approved in accordance with 14 CFR 21.305(d) approved for airplane use only. Car Seats: A child or kid between the ages of seven days and 2 years can use a car seat on Frontier Airlines flights. However, this is possible when there is enough room in the seat. The car seat can be utilized by any child with a separate seat. It must always be fastened with a seat belt. Booster Seats: According to the Frontier Airlines baggage allowance 2025, ​Booster seats are allowed on airplanes. However, during takeoff and landing, they must be stored beneath the seat or in an overhead compartment. A flier is permitted to utilize the seat during the trip. Further, when the airplane starts to descend, the booster seat needs to be stowed. Are Pets Permitted on Frontier Airlines Flights?For a one-way transportation charge, domesticated dogs, cats, rabbits, guinea pigs, hamsters, or small home birds can be transported on Frontier flights inside the United States of America. As per the Frontier Airlines international baggage allowance​ regulations, the pet must always remain in its travel container for safety reasons. According to CDC regulations, only service animals are permitted in the cabin while traveling internationally. The following are the allowances for pet travel containers: A hard-sided pet container’s maximum measurements are 8*14*18 inches.18 inches in length, 14 inches in width, and 11 inches in height are the maximum measurements for a soft-sided pet container. Frontier Airlines advises using soft-sided cases for all containers. Fliers are permitted to bring a carry-on bag or a personal item in addition to their pet travel containers. Frontier Airlines Baggage Allowance for Military Members 1 complimentary personal item is allowed to Military members on board. They are also allowed to bring a free carry-on bag. Further, 2 complimentary checked bags are permitted. All luggage must adhere to the weight and dimension restrictions specified by the baggage acceptance policies. Note: Overweight and oversized baggage penalties are waived for military members. How to Include Military Bags?Military members can include extra Frontier Airlines international baggage allowance​ at the airport or check-in gate. Here, they must show a legitimate, unexpired U.S. Government Common Access Card (CAC) with uniformed facilities or academy connection or an Armed Forces of the United States Identification Card with an active duty, reserve, guard, or academy affiliation. For the same procedure, there are no fees charged. Frontier Airlines Allowance for Special Assistance Scooters, wheelchairs, i.e. electric/manual, and other mobility aids are permitted objectives to be transported on Frontier Airlines planes in the passenger cabin or cargo area at no cost. The passenger compartment can accommodate up to two wheelchairs, depending on space. The aircraft’s cargo compartment will be used to store any extra wheelchairs. Further, wheelchairs are not allowed in the cabin when they are more than 40 inches high by 13 inches broad by 50 inches long. Additionally, according to the Frontier Airlines baggage allowance 2025 guidelines​, the weight should not exceed 70 pounds.
