Passengers who are familiar with these allowances in advance can confidently plan and pack their stuff for hassle-free travel domestically as well as internationally. What is the Carry-on Baggage Allowance for Delta Airlines International?Every traveler is allowed to bring one carry-on bag and one personal item at no additional cost as part of the baggage allowance for Delta Airlines International. For instance, a laptop bag, handbag, or other comparable object that can fit beneath the seat in front of them. The total length, width, and height of the carry-on bags cannot be more than 45 linear inches or 114 cm. Individual maximum measurements of the same can be 56 cm * 35 cm* 23 cm, respectively. These measurements include any handles or wheels. However, it does not apply to little musical instruments that are able to fit under or above seats. Further, in case passengers’ luggage does not fit in the overhead bins, the flight attendant can help. Note that due to lack of overhead space, travelers on Delta Connection flights, including those with 50 seats or fewer are only allowed to bring personal belongings on board. Items Allowed on Delta Airlines Carry-on Bags Even though passengers can bring a lot of items in their carry-on and personal belongings, they should think about the things that are restricted for their own protection. The majority of nations impose size restrictions on containers holding liquids, gels, aerosols, and pasted, even when the containers are partially empty. In a carry-on bag, the maximum liquids should be 3.4 ounces or 100 millimeters. When guests’ credit card or Medallion status changes, their Delta Airlines baggage allowance can too. They are allowed to bring one bag and 1 small personal item on flights operated by Delta for free. However, flights operated by carriers other than Delta may have different carry-on allowances and prices. Therefore, for specific restrictions and fees, get in touch with the operating carrier. What is Delta Airlines Checked Baggage Allowance?​The maximum Delta Airlines checked baggage allowance​ is 62 inches or 158 cm, including length, width, and height. On flights operated by Delta Connection, a maximum of 4 baggage can be checked. However, beyond these limits, passengers need to pay some costs as determined in the Delta Airlines baggage allowance policy. To avoid these fees, passengers can carry a specified allowance or become a member of the Delta SkyMiles Medallion program.
