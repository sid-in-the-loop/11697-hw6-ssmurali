Please note that collections and deliveries take place between 9 am and 6 pm, Monday to Friday, excluding public holidays.What type of Boxes should be used?To ensure a smooth shipping experience, we recommend using strong, double-walled cardboard boxes to package your personal belongings securely. You can add bubble wrap, newspaper, or other protective materials inside the box to keep your items safe during transit. While My Baggage does not supply packaging materials, you can purchase suitable boxes online. Here are some examples of common box sizes & weights we accept: 20KG: 45 x 45 x 45cm / 44lbs: 18 x 18 x 18in 30KG: 50 x 50 x 55cm / 66lbs: 20 x 20 x 22in Before shipping, please check that your box meets our requirements using our size checker. Avoid using crisp boxes or plastic storage containers, as they are often too fragile and may split during transit. For more detailed guidance, please visit our packaging page or contact our customer support team.What destinations can I ship my Boxes to?My Baggage offers a global shipping service to a wide range of international destinations. Whether you’re sending your boxes to Europe, the USA, or beyond, we’ve got you covered. To check if we deliver to your intended location, simply select your collection and delivery countries from the drop-down menus when getting an instant quote.Do my Boxes need to go through Customs?If you’re shipping boxes internationally, your shipment will need to go through customs in the destination country. A completed customs form is legally required for this process. Don’t worry - when you book with My Baggage, we’ll automatically generate the necessary customs forms for you to complete online. This saves you time and makes the process much easier. You’ll simply need to answer a few questions and declare the value of the items in your shipment. For domestic shipments or shipments within the same customs zone, such as within the EU, customs checks are not required, and no customs forms will be needed. Most My Baggage shipments clear customs quickly and are often free from duties and taxes, as many countries provide a generous duty-free allowance for personal effects. However, customs processes and allowances vary between countries, and My Baggage does not have direct links to customs authorities. We recommend researching the customs regulations of your destination country before booking your shipment to understand any potential duties or taxes. Be sure to follow all advice and requirements provided by the customs authority.
