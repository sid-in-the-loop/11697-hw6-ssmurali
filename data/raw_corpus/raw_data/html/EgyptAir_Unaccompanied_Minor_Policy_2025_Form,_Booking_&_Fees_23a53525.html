The Egyptair unaccompanied minor policy 2025 allows children older than five years to travel alone. Unaccompanied minors, often known as UMs, are permitted to travel with documentation that has been validated by the government. Further, according to this policy, certain fees must be paid before utilizing the service. It also has regulations for flying, picking up, dropping off, etc. These can be general or occasionally location-specific. Moreover, to book this service, parents or guardians can communicate with the officials of this airline over the phone or in person at the nearest office. Egyptair Unaccompanied Minor Rules for GeneralThe Egyptair unaccompanied minor rules include important travel standards that guarantee youngsters traveling alone are safe, comfortable, and under constant supervision.The juvenile or child is brought to the airport of departure by an authorized guardian.Further, they are required to stay at the airport terminal until the actual departure of the flight.The final decision about whether to allow unaccompanied minors or young travelers is made by EGYPTAIR, which also has the right to refuse their transport at any time, even after they have been accepted, if the escorting customers’ apparent conditions do not satisfy the requirements for escort travel.Based on the Egyptair unaccompanied minor rules, children who are visually or auditorily challenged are not permitted to travel alone.Children aged 6 to 12 years or guided minors aged 2 to 6 years must be booked as UM if they are traveling in a different cabin class than their guardians. ​ Depending on their age and if they need extra assistance with toileting, feeding, or giving medication, children can travel alone on the majority of journeys.Only Egyptair flights are subject to the aforementioned regulations.For code-share flights run by other airlines, parents can contact the call center or the Egyptair office that is closest to them.According to the unaccompanied minor policy for Egyptair, a member of the Egyptair ground crew will meet them at a designated time and take the child through immigration and customs to the departure gate. When the child lands, the Egyptair ground crew will be there to help with arrival procedures.After that, the crew will give the child to the person they designated to greet them.This individual has to present identification.Egyptair requires the parent or guardian to be present when the child is dropped off or picked up at the terminal for travel to and from the United States.Please consult the Egyptair policy on unaccompanied minors if parents have connecting flights on another carrier.When making a reservation, a parent or guardian fills out a declaration form to release Egyptair from any guardianship obligations and to supply the information for an escort upon arrival or departure.An unaccompanied minor’s passport, health certificate, and baggage identification tags may be handled by an escort or kept by the child, ideally in a wallet issued at the airport of departure. Moreover, they will make sure the child understands the significance of their tickets and passport, as well as their travel itinerary and who will be waiting for them when they arrive.
