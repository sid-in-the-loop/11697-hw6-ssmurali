due to equipment size or other operational constraints. SkyWest cannot accept kayaks, windsurf equipment, hang gliders, or extra-large or giant pet kennels. Also, any items classified as pressurized gases/liquids, oil-based paints, explosives, poisons, magnetic materials, flammable, corrosive, etc., are not accepted.All other partner baggage information applies as outlined on their site.
