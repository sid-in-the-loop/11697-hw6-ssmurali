Support Invest: SKYW Crew Support Career Guide apply now Become a SkyWest Crew Scheduler Looking for a fast-paced job with variety? As a Crew Scheduler you will work as part of a team supporting pilots and flight attendants across the country. This requires exceptional customer service, along with critical thinking and multi-tasking skills. In this role, you'll also enjoy numerous growth opportunities, robust training, and shift trade flexibility. The result is a rewarding opportunity to learn many different facets of the SkyWest operation. Requirements Must be able to follow written and verbal instruction Ability to work with others as part of a team and work under tight time constraints and in high-stress situations Good working knowledge of PCs and Windows applications is mandatory Familiarity with multi-line phone operations Must be able to work all shifts designated within crew support, including midnight shifts, weekends, and holiday schedules Exceptional customer service skills Excellent written and verbal communication skills Must be able to perform basic mathematical computations using both decimals and hours/minutes Good typing and ten-key skills Must possess excellent organizational skills, be a self-starter and work well as part of a team Essential Duties and Responsibilities Maintain necessary communication with all crewmembers, flight control and station personnel related to crew schedules Continuous auditing and verification of coverage of all scheduled flights Notification of changes to crewmember daily/monthly schedules Continuous monitoring of crewmember schedules to ensure legality/FAR compliance Secure all necessary accommodations, including, but not limited to, hotel and deadhead flight bookings Provide excellent customer service and support to all crewmembers through handling all scheduling related items, including relaying correct policy, procedural and regulatory information apply now Edit Application Support Quick Links Home Press Releases Route Map Careers EOE Website Accessibility Contact Privacy Terms Copyright Partners United Airlines Delta Air Lines Alaska Airlines American Airlines Media Press Releases Route Map Aircraft Story Ideas Image Gallery Affiliates Careers Open Jobs Career Guides Social Twitter Blog Glassdoor © 2025 SkyWest Airlines
