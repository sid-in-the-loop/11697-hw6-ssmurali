In addition to offering cheap flights, travelers can choose this air carrier due to its convenient baggage allowances. Spirit Airlines baggage allowance 2025 includes checked, personal, and carry-on bags while traveling domestically and internationally. In addition, medical equipment, sports gear, and other items are permitted. However, to carry these, some restrictions and rules can be applicable. These can be decided based on the type of fare, route, and more. When crossing these restrictions, some fees can be charged by the airline. To avoid these, make sure to carry stuff as per the allowed baggage allowance. Types of Spirit Airlines International Baggage Allowance Spirit Airlines has a range of baggage allowance choices to accommodate different traveler requirements. Personal belongings, checked, and carry-on bags are among them. In order to guarantee a convenient travel experience, this air operator also offers special baggage facilities, including musical instruments, athletic gear, and mobility aids. Baggage Allowance for Personal Item Each guest is permitted to carry 1 personal item, such as a purse or small backpack. It should completely fit in the lower-size bin. Along with this, he or she is allowed to bring free-of-charge goods, such as umbrellas, cameras, baby diaper bags, etc. With the handle and wheels included, the maximum dimensions should be 45 cm* 35 cm* 20 cm. Wheelchairs, strollers, and car seats can occasionally be checked at the gate or allowed free of charge. Note: Personal items do not include folding carts. Spirit Airline Carry-on Baggage Allowance​In addition to personal items, the Go Big, Go Comfy, and Go Savvy travel options allow for a carry-on bag with some dimensions. The maximum dimensions are 56 cm* 46 cm* 25 cm, with handles and wheels. Spirit Airline carry on baggage allowance​ is not included in the Go travel option, nor is it available for separate purchases. In case passengers need to add an extra carry-on bag to their flights, they can upgrade to Go Comfy or Go Big. Further, they will no longer be able to upgrade to Go Savvy when they purchase Go on or after February 5, 2025. Note: When a carry-on bag cannot be securely stored, the staff of this airline might need it to be checked. Checked Baggage Allowance The Go Big travel option includes checked luggage. Also, additional checked baggage can be added and purchased individually (space allowing). For the Go Savvy, Go Comfy, and Go travel choices, travelers can add checked baggage separately (space allowed behind the initial checked bag). Including handles and wheels, the maximum Spirit Airlines international baggage allowance​ is 62 linear inches (length + width + height, 158 cm). A maximum weight of 50 pounds or 22.6 kg is allowed. Further, any bag beyond these measurements is subject to fees. Note: Suitcases weighing more than 100 pounds/45 kg or larger than 80 linear inches/203 cm in total dimensions are not accepted. Baggage Allowance For Traveling with Children on Spirit Airlines For no additional fee, Spirit Airlines will gladly check 1 stroller and 1 car seat per child. Additionally, as long as a separate seat has been purchased for the child, passengers are permitted to bring an FAA-approved child restraint system (car seat) or an FAA-approved Child Harness Device (CARES) on board. Spirit Airlines Baggage Allowance for Military Members As part of Spirit Airlines baggage allowance​, 2 complimentary checked bags and 1 carry-on luggage to active U.S. military service members and their spouses and/or children is allowed. In addition, they are allowed to carry 1 free personal item. In case they need to add bags, the procedure should be made online either during the online check-in or after booking their tickets using the “My Trips” option on Spirit’s website. Spirit Airlines Pet Allowance on FlightsThe animal needs to be in a container that Spirit Air inspects and approves. 1 pet will be considered 1 piece of carry-on luggage. The container must fit beneath the seat in front of the guest who is traveling with the animal. 45.72 cm* 35.56 cm* 22.86 cm are the total dimensions of this container. It is important to verify that the container satisfies all legal standards for secure transit. Only soft containers are permitted in the cabin according to Spirit Airline carry on baggage allowance​​. The pet must be able to stand and turn around in the container and must always be kept in the carrier. Spirit Airlines Baggage Allowance Fees The cost of Spirit Airlines baggage allowance varies based on the kind of bag, travel date, methods used, and route. In relation to the same, the cost of extra bags can be around USD 30 to USD 100. Fees for Spirit Airline Carry-on Baggage Allowance​Based on when and where passengers pay, Spirit Airlines carry-on baggage allowance costs can range from USD 30 to USD 65 per bag. The lowest rates are available when paying online at the time of booking. Further, payments made at the airport can result in higher fees. At the time of booking, passengers can be charged USD 37 for 1 carry-on bag. USD 47 can be levied before online web check-in. At online web check-in, USD 47 can be charged. Spirit Air can charge USD 55 at the airport ticket counter. The fee for an extra Spirit Airline carry on baggage allowance​ at the airport gate is USD 65. USD 95 is levied when they add an excess bag at online check-in. At the airport ticket desk, USD 100 can be charged. How to Increase Bags on Spirit Airlines Flights Reservations?There are several ways available to increase or add Spirit Airlines international baggage allowance​. Online and offline are two ways available. As an offline mode, passengers can add bags over the phone. Or, they can come to the specific airport for the same. On the other hand, one can use the official website of Spirit Air as part of the online way. In case a passenger’s flight is more than 24 hours before departure, the following steps have to be followed: Come to the Spirit Air’s website. Choose the “My Trips” feature. Enter some information, including:“Last Name”“Confirmation Code”Hit on the “Continue” button. Following the steps, travelers can buy more bags by clicking the “Add Bags” link. By paying associated fees, an extra Spirit Airlines baggage allowance​ can be purchased. When a flight is less than 24 hours before departure, just select the Online Check-in option on the website for adding extra bag allowance and follow further steps. They can simply calculate by entering their flights and baggage information. This can save money at the airport as well as time.
