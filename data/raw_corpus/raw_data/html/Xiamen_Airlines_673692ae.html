Irregular Carriage of Baggage （1）General Provisions1) In the event of any delay, loss, damage or destruction of baggage carriage, the passenger should complete the baggage incident record and go through relevant procedures of Xiamen Airlines or its authorized ground handling agent at the place of the incident;2) If the passenger's checked baggage fails to arrive with the passenger due to any Xiamen Airlines reasons, and fails to be delivered to the passenger on the same day, resulting in inconvenience to passenger's journey life, the passenger can be compensated for temporary daily necessities at the following standard:Passenger classEconomy classBusiness class/Egret PlatinumFirst class/Egret DiamondCompensation for temporary daily necessitiesDomesticRMB 200RMB 300RMB 500InternationalRMB 300 or equivalent foreign currencyRMB 400 or equivalent foreign currencyRMB 500 or equivalent foreign currency（2） Compensation of Baggage1. Xiamen Airlines is not responsible for the destruction, loss, or damage of passengers' luggage caused entirely by the luggage's natural attributes, quality, or defects.2. In the event of damage, destruction, or loss of checked luggage on Xiamen Airlines flights, Xiamen Airlines will compensate or cover repair costs within the luggage compensation limit according to the value of the lost luggage:(1) The compensation liability limit for domestic flights is RMB 100 per kilogram. If the value of the luggage is less than RMB 100 per kilogram, the compensation shall be made according to the actual value. In case of damage to the luggage box, the compensation limit shall be calculated according to the weight of the luggage box itself.(2) For international and regional flights that meet the conditions of the Warsaw Convention, the compensation limit is 250 francs per kilogram; for those that meet the conditions of the Montreal Convention, the compensation limit for checked luggage is 1519 Special Drawing Rights per passenger. Passengers bear the burden of proof for the actual value of the loss, but not exceeding the compensation limit applicable under the international convention.3. The weight used to determine Xiamen Airlines' compensation liability limit for the destruction, loss, damage, or delay of checked luggage or any items in the luggage shall only be the actual weight of the damaged luggage or items; if the weight of the damaged luggage or items cannot be determined, the damaged luggage of each passenger can only be calculated based on the free luggage allowance enjoyed by the passenger.4. Important documents and materials, securities, cash, bills, jewelry, precious metals and their products, antiques, calligraphy and painting samples, or other valuable items, fragile or perishable items, prescription medications that need to be taken regularly, medical certificates, travel documents, and other items that require personal care cannot be checked in as luggage or placed inside checked luggage. For the destruction, loss, or damage of the aforementioned items placed within checked luggage, Xiamen Airlines will only assume liability for compensation as regular checked luggage.5. When handling luggage loss compensation, the compensation for temporary living supplies that has been paid shall be deducted from the compensation amount, and the excess luggage fees charged for the compensated luggage shall be refunded.6. For domestic segments that constitute international transportation, luggage compensation shall be handled according to the applicable international transportation luggage compensation regulations.7. If the compensated lost luggage is found, Xiamen Airlines will notify the passenger as soon as possible. Passengers can reclaim their luggage and return the full compensation, but the temporary living supplies compensation will not be refunded. If it is found that the passenger has obvious fraudulent behavior, Xiamen Airlines has the right to recover the full compensation.8. In case of loss of passengers' luggage, passengers shall submit a written objection to Xiamen Airlines or its authorized ground service agent.
