Baggage allowances are forever changing. The amount of luggage you are allowed to carry is getting smaller, and new charges are constantly being imposed. It is difficult to know exactly where you stand as a passenger. That is why Send My Bag® has done the hard work for you and put together a guide on the baggage allowances and restrictions you can expect from the main airlines. Click on an icon below to discover exactly what you are allowed to bring into the cabin, how many bags you can check into the hold, and, most importantly, how much you will be charged to do so.If you are considering whether or not to take luggage with you on your flight, bear in mind the recent announcement from IATA that airlines may soon introduce even smaller hand baggage restrictions.
