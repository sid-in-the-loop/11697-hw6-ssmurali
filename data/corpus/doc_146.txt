With My Baggage, you don’t need to worry about this process! When you book with us, we automatically generate the necessary customs paperwork for you to complete.Below we’ve outlined important customs information for shipping personal effects to the USA. Whilst we can provide you with general customs guidance, please be aware that regulations can change unexpectedly and may vary based on your shipment and individual circumstances. To ensure you have the most accurate and up-to-date information, we strongly recommend conducting your own research for shipments to America.Will my shipment to the USA go through Customs?International shipments to the USA will be subject to US Customs Border Patrol (CBP) checks, and require a completed customs form. My Baggage will generate these forms for you based on the information provided during your order. You can access and complete your customs forms in the ‘My Orders’ section of our website once your order has been paid for.What is the Customs processing time for shipments to the USA?Each shipment to the USA is unique and will be individually inspected by US Customs. Clearance times can vary based on several factors. If your customs paperwork is completed and submitted correctly, your shipment with My Baggage should clear customs promptly and arrive on the estimated delivery date.What documentation is required for shipping personal effects to the USA?When shipping to the USA, in addition to customs forms we provide, you’ll need to include some extra documentation.This includes:• A copy of the receiver’s passport image page• A CBP3299 form - we will include this along with your customs formsUS Customs may request more information based on your individual circumstances. If anything additional is required, our courier partners will contact you.To prevent any potential issues, it's important that all customs documentation is in the name of the receiver, who should be the owner of the shipment. If any paperwork is in the sender’s name and differs from the receiver’s name, customs may reject the shipment.When shipping personal effects, please ensure that both the sender’s and receiver’s names match the name of the shipment's owner.Do I need to declare the value of my items when shipping to the USA?For shipments to the USA you are required to declare the value of your items on your customs forms.When completing your customs forms, it’s important that you do this accurately and clearly. In order to demonstrate that your shipment consists of personal effects, you must describe each item within your shipment. For example, you might declare:4 x Used Black Men's Cotton T-Shirts1 x Pair Used Brown Leather Shoes (size 8)2 x Used Dark Blue Jeans Denim (size Small)Will I need to pay Customs fees for shipments to the USA?When shipping personal effects to the USA, customs fees occasionally apply depending on various factors, including the accuracy of the declared values on your customs forms. Any fees can be paid online or by phone through our courier partner, who will work with US Customs to expedite the clearance process and ensure your shipment is delivered as quickly as possible.Maintaining a low customs value on your items may reduce the risk of incurring customs duties/taxes.Shipping Gifts to the USAIf you’re sending a gift to friends or family at a residential address in the USA, your gift can be up to the value of $100 USD, meaning it’ll be free from customs duty or tax - as long as the same person does not receive more than $100 worth of gifts in a single dayGifts for more than one person may be shipped in the same package, called a “consolidated gift package”. They must be individually wrapped and labelled with each recipient’s name.If the gifts are shipped from an insular possession (US associated jurisdiction) this amount is increased to $200.Should I consider anything else when shipping to the USA?It’s important not to send Medical Equipment or Food items to the USA on our service. Sending such items may result in your shipment being returned to the original collection address, and subsequent return fees being charged.Please note: US Customs consider Sunglasses, Eye-glasses, and Contact Lenses to be Medical Equipment.We aim to deliver shipments to the USA within the estimated delivery time provided when you place your order. However, delays can occasionally occur, so we don’t recommend sending time-sensitive items.Important Information for Shipments to AmericaIt’s very important that you review this information carefully if you’re shipping personal effects or gifts to the United States of America. Please be aware that you must not send any Prohibited Items to America. Some items are classified as Non-Compensation Items, which can be shipped, but at your own risk.
