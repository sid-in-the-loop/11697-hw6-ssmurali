Will my shipment have to go through Canadian customs?All international shipments into Canada will be subject to customs inspections and require a completed customs declaration form. When you book with My Baggage, we'll automatically generate a digital customs form for your shipment to Canada based on the information you provide during your order. You can conveniently complete this in the 'My Orders' section of our website shortly after your order has been paid - you'll just have to answer a few questions and provide a copy of your passport. Customs may request additional information or documentation to support your shipment to Canada. Customs fees and duties may apply, which will be payable to Canadian customs via our courier partner. Shipping personal effects to Canada requires you to be physically present in the country in order for the shipment to be cleared. It may be necessary to visit a local customs office to collect your shipment, to which a customs clearance fee of $16 CAD will be payable. On rare occasions, you may be required to hire a customs broker. We don't recommend sending personal effects to Canada if it's for short-term travel purposes, including holidays. It's more suitable for those relocating to Canada for work or study. For more detailed information or information specific to your shipment and circumstances, we suggest that you contact Canadian customs directly.
