We recommend pre-paying for your checked and/or carry-on bags during your initial reservation to save time and money. Any Customer that is an active-duty military member on official or personal travel and presents a current active-duty military identification card may bring one (1) carry-on bag and check two (2)* checked bags for free, and not be subject to excess, oversize, or overweight baggage charges. *Checked baggage allowances may be subject to embargoes on certain flights: For flights with a checked baggage limit of one (1) checked baggage per person, active-duty military members are entitled to only one (1) free checked bag and one (1) free carry-on bag. Please see ‘Checked bag’ information below for a list of cities and effective dates. Examples of personal items include a purse, briefcase, laptop computer case, small backpack or small camera case. Overhead bin carry-on bag 22″L x 14″H x 9″W From $45 to $77 One carry-on bag is permitted in the aircraft cabin for a fee. The carry-on bag must fit into an overhead bin. Examples of a carry-on bag include a roller bag, garment bag, large backpack, tote bag – in all cases capable of being safely stowed in an overhead bin. Checked bag 62″ total & up to 50 lbs From $42 to $77 Checked bags may weigh up to 50 pounds (22 kilograms) and measure up to 62 inches (157 cm) in overall exterior dimensions (length + width + height) including handles and wheels. Any checked baggage that exceeds these limits will be subject to additional excess baggage charges. Each Customer may check up to ten (10) bags on domestic flights and one (1) bag on international flights, for a fee per bag. Oversized and overweight bags Baggage in excess of 62 inches up to 80 inches (158 cm to 203 cm) in overall exterior dimensions (length + width + height) and/or baggage weighing over 50 pounds up to 70 pounds (22.8 kg to 31.8 kg).
